project_stack = ["Python", "Flask", "PostgreSQL", "HTML", "CSS", "WTForms"]


project_description = "While in the SaaS sales industry, I learned how beneficial it is for sales representatives to connect their prospects with current satisfied customers -- also known as sales advocates -- for reference calls. Doing this greatly increases the chance of a sale, but it can be difficult to pull off, mainly due to reasons such as not having a process in place, scheduling conflicts, or simply that the advocate is not incentivized to meet. I am creating a scheduling app that solves these problems.

Using Kredible, sales representatives can view the current availability of their advocates and request meetings. Sales advocates, in turn, can log in, view and accept these reference calls, and are incentivized to do so by receiving gift cards in exchange for completed meetings."
